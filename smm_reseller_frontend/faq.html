How do I place an order? +
Go to the services page, select a category, choose a service, enter your link or username, and click “Place Order”.
How long does delivery take? +
Delivery time varies depending on the service. Most start instantly or within minutes, while others may take a few hours.
What happens if my order does not deliver? +
You can contact our support team and provide your order ID. We will check and either refill or refund where required.
Do you offer refunds? +
Refunds are issued only for orders that cannot be delivered. Completed orders are non-refundable.
Are the services safe? +
Yes, our services are tested, stable, and safe for use. We do not require login details for any order.
