Common Questions

What's the meaning of average/high quality?
Average quality means your order uses standard accounts or likes that may not be premium, but deliver fast and reliable results. High quality uses more active or verified accounts, giving better engagement and long-lasting results. High quality usually costs a bit more but ensures safer and more stable delivery.
Why do my orders take so long?
Orders can take time due to network delays, platform limits, or large order volumes. Social networks may also throttle actions to prevent spam. Most orders start within minutes, but some may take several hours to fully process, depending on the service and the target audience.
Will my account get banned if I artificially boost my followers/likes?
Our services are designed to minimize risk, but there is always a small chance if the platform detects unusual activity. We use safe delivery methods with gradual engagement to reduce the chance of bans. We recommend following platform rules and not combining boosts with risky automated tools.
Why do my orders keep getting cancelled and refunded?
Orders may be cancelled automatically if the target profile is private, deleted, or invalid. Platforms may also remove fake accounts or bots. Refunds are provided for failed orders, and we always aim to reprocess or refill affected services whenever possible.
