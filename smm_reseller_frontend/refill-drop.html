Boosting Glove Support

Refill / Drop

Need help with a refill or drop? Check the FAQs below or contact our support team directly for assistance.

How do I request a refill?

To request a refill, contact support with your order ID and details of the service you want refilled. We will process it as quickly as possible.

My drop didn’t reflect

If your drop hasn’t appeared, verify your username and order details, then contact support to resolve the issue.

Can I cancel a refill/drop?

Once a refill or drop has started, it cannot be canceled. Contact support if there is an urgent issue.

How long does a refill/drop take?

Most refills and drops are processed within the warranty period, depending on the service purhased.

I received the wrong refill/drop

Contact support immediately with your order ID and the issue. We will verify and correct it as soon as possible.
