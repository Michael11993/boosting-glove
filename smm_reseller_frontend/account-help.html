Account Help

Need help with your account? Find answers to common account-related questions below or contact our support team directly for assistance.

How do I reset my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email to receive a password reset link. Follow the instructions to set a new password.

How do I update my email or username?

Currently, email and username changes must be requested through our support team. Contact us via WhatsApp with your account details.

My account is locked or banned

If your account is locked or banned, contact support with your account details. We will review your case and provide guidance.
