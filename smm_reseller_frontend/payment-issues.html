Payment Issues

Having trouble with your payment? Find answers to common payment-related problems below or contact our support team for assistance.

My payment didn’t go through

Ensure that your payment details are correct and that your account has sufficient funds. If it still fails, contact support via WhatsApp with your payment reference.

I was charged but my order didn’t start

Sometimes payments take time to reflect. Contact support with your payment confirmation and order details to resolve the issue.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled according to our policies. Contact support with your order ID and reason to request a refund.

My payment was deducted twice

Contact support immediately with your payment proof. We will verify and correct any double charges.
