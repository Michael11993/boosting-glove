Order Issues

Having trouble with your order? Find answers to common order-related problems below or contact our support team for assistance.

My order hasn’t started yet, what should I do?

If your order hasn’t started within the expected time, please check your payment confirmation first. If it’s confirmed, contact our support team via WhatsApp for help.

Some of my order wasn’t delivered

If part of your order is missing, contact support immediately. Provide your order ID and details so we can investigate and resolve it.

How do I cancel an order?

Orders cannot be canceled once started. If you need a refund or adjustment, contact our support team for assistance.

I received the wrong service/package

Contact support with your order ID. We will verify and correct the issue as quickly as possible.

My order is delayed

Delays can occur due to high demand or social media platform issues. Contact support for updates or possible solutions.
